Of Steam and Power

Of Steam and Power or OSAP is a TTRPG set in the late industrial revolution where technology has never been better, the rich continue to exploit more and more of the cogs and Steampunkers possess the unique ability to generate power and electricity - an unique ability that accelerated technology so far, the people you meet never seem to be quite organic humans...

WHAT IS OF STEAM AND POWER?

Of Steam and Power brings modern and futuristic technology and combines it with the classic steam-based society of the Industrial Revolution. Perfect for both beginners and veterans of the TTRPG genre, Of Steam and Power is heavily inspired by Dungeons and Dragons and Cyberpunk series. Of Steam and Power has 5 different abilities (Charisma, Strength/Body, Dexterity/Reflexes, Technology and Intelligence), your characters are different in proficiencies, traits and unique skillsets. For each ability and class, there will be a skill tree progression system - the more experienced your characters are, the more effective and skilled your characters are in their respective skillset.

PLAY THE GAME HOW YOU WANT

Of Steam and Power allows for the GMs of parties to create their own campaigns, rulesets and items. We provide the core, you provide the creativity.
